Social media refers to websites and applications that are designed to allow people to share content quickly and easily. Most people would explain social media as apps on their smartphone or laptop. Most people would use social media as apps on their phones. With social media we can share photos, opinions, events, etc in real-time. This has changed the way we live, nearly everyone uses social media in their everyday life. Social media is even used in businesses. Social media can be used as a marketing tool, businesses promote themselves to their followers on social media platfroms. There are many different types of social media available on the internet and they can be used in many different ways by anyone, anywhere.
