Social media and technology are part of most students everyday life. Social media platforms come with many benefits as well. Such as...

    1. They have the facility to hold live lectures.
    2. They allow the lecturers to communicate easily with students and share announcements.
    3. Students can communicate with their peers easily outside of college, they can form group chats for projects and communicate there by sharing work or giving ideas.
    4. Social media platforms allows students to have a discussion about the work, college assignments etc.
    5. Social media can help universities share photos of events to their followers and showcase what they offer.
    6. Universities can also link their social media pages with their website which creates more of a following for them.
    7. Social media helps students collaborate with one another and creates an place for brilliant interactive education.
